    200g of beef liver
    Flour, for dredging
    Eggs, beaten
    Panko bread crumbs
    Oil, for frying

Instructions:

    Cut the beef liver into thin slices.
    Dredge the slices in flour, then dip them in beaten eggs, and finally coat them with panko bread crumbs.
    Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
    Fry the slices until they are golden brown on both sides.
    Drain on paper towels and serve hot.

3. Beef Liver Stroganoff:
Ingredients:

    200g of beef liver
    1/2 cup of dry white wine
    1/4 cup of sour cream
    2 tablespoons of flour
    Water
    Salt and pepper, to taste
    Butter
    Onion, diced
    Mushrooms, sliced
    Noodles, cooked

Instructions:

    Slice the beef liver into thin strips.
    In a large skillet, melt some butter over medium-high heat.
    Add the onion and mushrooms and cook until softened.
    Add the beef liver and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    Deglaze the pan with the white wine, scraping the bottom of the pan to remove any browned bits.
    Reduce the wine by half.
    Mix the sour cream and flour in a small bowl, then add to the pan, stirring constantly until thickened.
    Add water to thin out the sauce, if needed.
    Season with salt and pepper.
    Serve over cooked noodles.
